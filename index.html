Dr. Sima Kavand, MD Internal Medicine & Derm

Dr. Sima Kavand is a board-certified internist and an international fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology. She earned her medical degree at Tehran University of Medical Sciences and completed a four-year dermatology residency in Iran. Before relocating to the United States, she served as an esteemed dermatology consultant.

In the US, she first worked as a postdoctoral research associate in the department of dermatology at the University of Minnesota. She then completed her internal medicine residency at the Saint Francis hospital affiliated with the University of Illinois.

Dr. Kavand's unique qualifications allow her to understand the complexity of internal diseases and their manifestations as skin disorders. She is also interested in preventive medicine and caring for patients with chronic skin conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, warts, and skin cancers. Patients can trust her to not only treat their skin, hair, and nail disorders but also to diagnose, treat, and prevent internal diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, and hyperlipidemia.
